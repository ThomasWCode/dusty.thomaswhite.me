About Dusty

Dusty is a cat. He is very busy.

He enjoys eating, roaming, and sleeping - not necessarily in that order.

Favourite Things

Food: Highly processed food only for some reason... except bacon. Oh, and dreamies.

Sleeping spots: Literally in the middle of the floor or stairs. Like the warm bathroom in winter.

Roaming routes: The many gardens and across roads, dispite our efforts to stop him.

Entertainment: The wide expanse of our neighbourhood's gardens.

Least favourite things: Any fresh food like chicken or fish... yh idk either, closed doors, and the vacuum cleaner.
